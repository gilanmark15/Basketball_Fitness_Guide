About This Program

The Basketball Performance Program is designed to provide athletes with a structured, essential blueprint for **strength and conditioning (S&C)**. It moves beyond general fitness to focus specifically on the physical traits that win games on the court.

Our Philosophy

Basketball is a game of **explosive movements** (jumping and sprinting) followed by **short rest periods**. Therefore, our training prioritizes:

  • **Vertical Power:** Training the legs to jump higher and more frequently (Plyometrics).
  • **Anaerobic Endurance:** Ensuring you maintain peak speed and quickness in the 4th quarter.
  • **Injury Resilience:** Strengthening the stabilizing muscles around the knees, ankles, and shoulders to withstand rapid acceleration and change of direction.
  • **Skill Integration:** Combining court-based shooting drills with physical conditioning to ensure your mechanics hold up under fatigue.

About This Website

This website is built using only **HTML and CSS** to ensure maximum compatibility and speed. We focused on keeping the structure intentionally simple:

  • **Simple Navigation:** The main menu directs you to separate HTML pages for each routine, avoiding complex loading times or code.
  • **Responsive Design:** The layout is designed to be easily readable and navigable whether you are viewing it on a large computer monitor or quickly checking a drill on a mobile phone during a workout.
  • **Core Goal:** To deliver actionable training information instantly, without technical distractions.
